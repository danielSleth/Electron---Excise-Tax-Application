Instructions

- Fill all the boxes for the excise tax report of the chosen month.

- For the Production Report, the relevant Cell in the source (Excel report) is displayed in the empty boxes.

- Taxes rates are to be updated annually. A value change in these boxes generates an annual adjustment.

- Upon completing data entry for chosen month, results appear in the tab "Excise Taxes". Earlier months are available.
